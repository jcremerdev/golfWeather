Golf Weather

Tips For Playin in Poor Conditions

Wind

For every 10mph of wind into your face, hit one club higher.

  • Ex. If you have a 150 yd shot with a 10 mph head wind, hit a 6 iron if you usually use a 7 for that distance.

Cold Weather

Play with more club, keep your hands and head warm and walk the course. Having a good attitude is a bit easier if you're a tad bit warmer!

Rain

Pack a dry towel and keep your clubs covered. The quality of the connection between you and your club is paramount to your success

Don't!

Find some place warmer and go there! Golf Weather's API offers weather data Worldwide... Search away!
